Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0


Innovation

Bangladesh fosters growth through innovation, driving economic progress with technology and creative solutions.

sustainability

The nation focuses on sustainability, adopting green technologies and resource conservation for long-term stability.

Infrastructure

Rapid development in transportation, energy, and communication enhances connectivity and supports economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh promotes equality, providing access to education, healthcare, and opportunities for all.

The vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to be a global leader in innovation, sustainability, infrastructure, and empowerment. The nation's vision is to create a prosperous, inclusive, and sustainable society that fosters growth, fosters innovation, and fosters empowerment.



Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Mordern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
